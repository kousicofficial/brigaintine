Maximum Versatility

M Line

The industry's most flexible plate technology for medium duties. High reliability and ease of maintenance for Australian manufacturing.

Unmatched Process Agility

The M Line gasketed plate-and-frame heat exchangers are designed for general industrial processes where flexibility and durability are critical. With a medium-sized frame and a wide range of plate patterns, the M Line can be tailored to meet your specific process requirements with extreme precision.

From simple water cooling to complex chemical reactions, the M Line delivers the performance stability that Australian industry relies on for safety and quality assurance.

BRIGANTINE M LINE

Industrial Grade Engineering

Optimized Plate Patterns

Features a variety of pressing patterns that allow for optimized flow paths and thermal lengths, ensuring perfect efficiency for your unique process.

Robust Frame Design

Highly durable carbon or stainless steel frames built to withstand industrial environments and simplify the plate pack opening/closing process.

Uniform Seal Integrity

State-of-the-art gasket technology that ensures long-term tightness even during frequent cleaning cycles and variable thermal loads.

Technical Advantages

  • Wide Range of Connection Sizes (up to 12")
  • Highly Scalable Frame Technology
  • Multiple Material Options (SS316, 254SMO)
  • Low Internal Hold-up Volume
  • Superior Fatigue Resistance

Operational Highlights

The M Line provides a balanced approach to industrial thermal design:

  • Cost-Effective Solution for Medium Duties
  • Easy Field Expansion (Add Plates Anytime)
  • Low Maintenance Requirements
  • Predictable Performance Benchmarking

Common Industrial Applications

General Manufacturing

Strategic cooling of hydraulic oils, equipment jackets, and production line processes in Australian factories.

Water Purification

Efficient thermal control for RO systems and large-scale water treatment infrastructure.

Food & Beverage

Pasteurization and thermal processing for dairies, breweries, and fruit juice production across Australia.

Mining Surface Support

Reliable cooling for generators and support infrastructure in remote Australian mining operations.
