// Service Overview

Specialist Compabloc Maintenance for High-Demand Duty

The Compabloc fully-welded plate heat exchanger is engineered for extreme operating conditions where gasketed alternatives are unsuitable. As an authorised Alfa Laval service partner, Brigantine Engineering delivers precision maintenance services that restore thermal efficiency and extend equipment service life across Australia.

Our engineers are trained in the unique service requirements of welded PHEs, applying specialist diagnostic techniques and proprietary tooling unavailable to non-authorised service providers.

// Technical Services

Service Capabilities

Welded plate heat exchangers present unique service challenges. Our specialist capability addresses every technical requirement with precision tooling and certified procedures.

01
Thermal Performance Diagnosis

Advanced fouling analysis and thermal benchmarking to quantify efficiency losses against design parameters. Identifies blockages, scaling, and channelling within the plate stack.

02
High-Pressure CIP Cleaning

Controlled chemical cleaning-in-place protocols using approved reagents compatible with welded stainless steel construction. Restores full plate channel flow.

03
Weld Integrity Inspection

Non-destructive weld inspection using certified NDT methods to identify micro-cracks, stress corrosion, or fatigue damage in the plate pack and shell welds.

04
Connection Overhaul

Inspection and reconditioning of inlet/outlet nozzles, flanges, and connections. Replacement of valve seats and sealing faces to restore leak-free service.

05
Pressure and Leak Testing

Full hydrostatic and pneumatic pressure testing following any repair or cleaning activity. Test certificates issued to Australian standards.

06
Emergency Rapid Response

Priority mobilisation for critical unplanned outages. Brigantine Engineering maintains an emergency service team for rapid deployment nationwide.

// Specialist Expertise

Why Compabloc Demands Specialists

The Case for Authorised Service

The Compabloc's fully-welded plate stack eliminates external gaskets, making it ideal for high-temperature, high-pressure, and aggressive media duties. However, this same design makes improper servicing attempts potentially catastrophic.

  • OEM-approved service procedures and specialist tooling
  • Trained and certified technicians specific to Compabloc design
  • Access to genuine Alfa Laval spare parts and components
  • Post-service documentation with full traceability

Extreme Duty Reliability

Compabloc welded PHEs operate in the most demanding sectors of Australian industry where standard equipment fails. Our services ensure your critical assets maintain integrity under extreme loads.

  • Oil and gas processing and refinery applications
  • Chemical and petrochemical plant duties
  • High-pressure steam and condensate recovery
  • Pharmaceutical and sterile process industries
