Ziepack

The ultimate hybrid heat exchanger for condensation and evaporation. Flexible technical performance for Australian chemical manufacturing.

Advanced Hybrid Technology for Extreme Efficiency

Ziepack is a hybrid heat exchanger that combines the advantages of traditional shell-and-tube units with the high thermal efficiency of plate-and-shell technology. Its unique design allows for extremely low pressure drops, making it perfect for vacuum condensation and other intensive thermal duties.

In Australian specialty chemical and pharmaceutical plants, Ziepack units are trusted for their robust performance, ease of maintenance, and ability to handle high flow rates in a remarkably compact design.
