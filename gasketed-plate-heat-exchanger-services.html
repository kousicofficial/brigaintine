Expert PHE Servicing for Continuous Industrial Operations

Brigantine Engineering provides comprehensive gasketed plate heat exchanger maintenance and restoration services across Australian industrial facilities. Our certified technicians apply exacting standards to every unit, ensuring thermal efficiency, leak-free performance, and extended asset life.

Whether performing a routine inspection, emergency regasketing, or a full overhaul, we apply factory-level precision with locally delivered service responsiveness. All work complies with Australian engineering standards and OEM specifications.

// What We Deliver

Service Capabilities

Our full-spectrum PHE service capabilities address every stage of your equipment lifecycle - from preventive maintenance through to emergency restoration.

01
🔬
Precision Inspection and Assessment

Complete dimensional and integrity inspection of plates, gaskets, frame components, and connection ports with detailed photographic condition reports.

02
🔄
Regasketing and Gasket Supply

Replacement of worn or failed gaskets using OEM-approved elastomers. Correct gasket grade selection for your specific media, temperature, and pressure conditions.

03
🧪
Chemical and Mechanical Cleaning

CIP assessment plus manual chemical cleaning and mechanical descaling of plates to restore full thermal transfer efficiency across all operating zones.

04
📐
Plate Straightening and Refurbishment

Correction of deformed or damaged plates to OEM specifications, eliminating cross-contamination pathways through systematic plate integrity restoration.

05
⚙️
Hydraulic Pressure Testing

Full hydrostatic pressure testing post-assembly to confirm leak-free operation before recommissioning. Certificates issued for all tested units.

06
📊
Performance Optimisation Audit

Thermal performance benchmarking against original design parameters with plate arrangement optimisation to maximise heat transfer effectiveness.
