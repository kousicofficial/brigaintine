// Service Overview

Specialist High-Speed Separator Servicing

High-speed disk stack separators operate at extreme rotational velocities under continuous process duty. Minor wear or imbalance at these speeds can manifest as vibration, performance decline, or catastrophic failure. Brigantine Engineering delivers authorised, factory-trained servicing for Alfa Laval disk stack separators across Australian industry.

From routine planned maintenance through to full bowl overhaul, our technicians bring OEM documentation, precision tooling, and genuine parts to every engagement, ensuring your separator returns to specification.

// Service Scope

Technical Service Scope

Our complete high-speed separator service capability addresses all critical subsystems, from rotating bowl assemblies to control systems.

01
Bowl & Disc Stack Inspection

Complete bowl disassembly, dimensional inspection of bowl body, distributor, and paring disc. Disc stack assessment and replacement.

02
Cleaning & Descaling

Manual and chemical cleaning of bowl internals to remove process deposits. Restoration of discharge nozzle geometry and flow paths.

03
Main Bearing Replacement

Replacement of vertical spindle and horizontal bearings. Correct pre-load settings and lubrication applied per OEM specifications.

04
Mechanical Seal Overhaul

Disassembly and replacement of mechanical seal faces, O-rings, and springs. Verification of seal face lapping and gap geometry.

05
Drive System Inspection

Inspection of worm gear, flat belt or direct drive systems. Belt tension adjustment and gear oil analysis for reliability.

06
Dynamic Acceptance Testing

Full test run with vibration monitoring, bearing temperature measurement, and separation efficiency verification.
