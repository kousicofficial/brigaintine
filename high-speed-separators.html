Why Choose Our High-Speed Separators

A proven methodology for restoring centrifugal separation performance

01

Inspection & Dismantling

The separator is fully disassembled; bowl, disc stack, bearings, and housing are inspected for wear, cracks, imbalance, corrosion, and dimensional accuracy.

02

Cleaning & Surface Preparation

All parts are deep-cleaned, degreased, grit-blasted, and prepared for restoration. Disc stacks and critical surfaces are precision-cleaned for optimal performance.

03

Repair of Bowl, Disc Stack & Shafts

Damaged surfaces are rebuilt, hardfaced, or machined. Disc stacks are repaired or replaced. Shafts, bearings, and seals are overhauled to meet OEM tolerances.

04

Balancing & Reassembly

The bowl, disc stack, and rotating assembly are dynamically balanced for smooth operation. The unit is reassembled with new bearings, seals, gaskets, and precision alignment.

05

Final Testing & Quality Check

The reconditioned separator undergoes full-speed test runs checking vibration, flow performance, temperature, noise, pressure, and discharge ‚Äî ensuring peak performance before dispatch.
