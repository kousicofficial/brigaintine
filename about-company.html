About Company

A legacy of engineering excellence in Australia. Delivering world-class industrial separation and heat transfer technology.

Technical Excellence Since Inception

Precision Engineering Across Australia

Brigantine Engineering Australia stands at the forefront of the industrial landscape, providing specialized services and products for heat transfer and separation processes. Based in Matraville, NSW, we serve a wide range of sectors across the continent.

Our commitment to precision engineering and sustainable solutions drives everything we do, from basic maintenance to complex system integration, ensuring your equipment operates at peak efficiency.
