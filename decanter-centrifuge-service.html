// Service Overview

Full-Spectrum Decanter Centrifuge Servicing

Decanter centrifuges are precision machines operating under demanding mechanical loads. Brigantine Engineering delivers factory-standard servicing for Alfa Laval decanter centrifuges across Australia, restoring separation performance and extending the operational life of your investment.

From scheduled preventive maintenance programmes to emergency breakdown response, our technicians operate with OEM-trained expertise, proprietary diagnostic tools, and genuine spare parts inventory.

// Technical Scope

Specialist Service Capabilities

Our decanter centrifuge service capability covers all critical mechanical, electrical, and process performance aspects of your equipment.

01
Mechanical Inspection

Precision dimensional measurement of bowl, scroll conveyor, bearings, and gear box components against OEM tolerances. Identifies wear before failure occurs.

02
Bowl and Scroll Overhaul

Complete bowl disassembly, scroll flight condition assessment, wear tile replacement, and reassembly to OEM clearance specifications.

03
Bearing and Seal Restoration

Replacement of main bearings, radial seals, and mechanical seals using genuine manufacturer parts. Correct pre-load procedures applied during reassembly.

04
Dynamic Balancing

Precision dynamic balancing of bowl and scroll assemblies after overhaul to eliminate vibration and protect bearing longevity.

05
Gearbox Servicing

Backdrive gearbox disassembly, gear and bearing inspection, oil analysis, and reconditioning to maintain precise differential speed control.

06
Performance Commissioning

Post-service functional testing under operational parameters with vibration monitoring and throughput verification before sign-off.
