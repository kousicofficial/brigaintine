Packinox

The world’s largest and most powerful plate heat exchanger. Unrivaled capacity for Australia's massive refinery projects.

Maximum Recovery, Minimum Footprint

Packinox heat exchangers are the definitive choice for refinery combined feed/effluent duties. Used primarily in continuous catalytic reforming (CCR), hydrotreating, and hydrocracking, they handle massive flow rates while providing the highest energy recovery in the industry.

Each Packinox unit is a custom-engineered solution, designed to operate at pressures up to 100 bar and temperatures up to 600°C. In the Australian mining and refining sectors, Packinox reliability is critical for minimizing energy costs and CO2 emissions.
