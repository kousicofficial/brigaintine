Industrial Solutions

01

Plate Heat Exchangers

Plate heat exchangers are compact devices used for efficient heat transfer between two fluids. Consisting of corrugated metal plates with gaskets, they create multiple channels for fluids to flow, maximizing surface area. Ideal for diverse applications, these exchangers offer rapid heating or cooling, energy efficiency, and easy maintenance.

02

Welded Heat Exchangers

A robust design with plates permanently welded together. Eliminating gaskets enhances durability and prevents leakage. These exchangers excel in high-pressure and high-temperature environments, ensuring reliable heat transfer. Their compact size, efficiency, and resistance to thermal stress make them suitable for various industrial processes.

03

Disk Stack Separators

Disk stack separators efficiently separate liquids with varying densities in industries like food, beverage, and oil. Consisting of stacked, rotating disks, they generate high centrifugal forces that separate solids and liquids. Compact and versatile, these separators offer precise control over separation processes, ensuring optimal product quality and efficiency.

04

Decanter Centrifuges

Used for liquid-solid separation. Featuring a rotating bowl and conveyor, they effectively separate components with different densities. Commonly employed in wastewater treatment and various industries, decanters provide efficient and continuous separation, contributing to enhanced process performance.
