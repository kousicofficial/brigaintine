Engineered for Aggressive Media

Industrial
Semi-Welded Line

Maximum safety for refrigeration and chemical duties. Laser-welded cassettes for total containment of hazardous fluids.

Compromise-Free Safety & Performance

The Industrial Semi-Welded Line is the ultimate solution for duties involving aggressive fluids, high pressures, or refrigerants like ammonia. By laser-welding two plates into a "cassette," we create a welded channel for the critical fluid, while keeping the other side gasketed for easy cleaning.

In Australian refrigeration and petrochemical sites, where environmental safety and operational uptime are paramount, our Semi-Welded technology provides an extra layer of protection without sacrificing thermal efficiency.

BRIGANTINE SEMI-WELDED

Advanced Safety Features

RefTight™ Sealing System

High-performance gasket grooves designed for maximum support, ensuring gas-tight sealing even under severe thermal cycles and high-pressure differentials.

Laser-Welded Cassettes

State-of-the-art laser welding technology minimizes risk of leakage for aggressive media, creating a durable, hermetically sealed channel.

Dynamic Leak Detection

Optimized drainage channels and gasket designs that provide early visual indication of any seal compromise, preventing cross-contamination.

Technical Advantages

  • Hermetic Containment for Hazardous Media
  • Superior Mechanical Strength & Durability
  • High Pressure Handling (Up to 63 Bar)
  • Reduced Gasket Surface Area
  • Flexible Connectivity Options

Operational Benefits

The Semi-Welded Line is designed for mission-critical operations:

  • Minimum Risk of Environmental Leakage
  • Simplified Maintenance for Gasketed Side
  • Longer Service Life vs Fully Gasketed
  • Optimized for Modern Refrigerants (R717)

Targeted Applications

Industrial Refrigeration

Ideal for ammonia (NH3) cascades, CO2 cooling, and refrigeration systems in Australian food and beverage processing.

Chemical Processing

Safe handling of aggressive chemicals, acids, and solvents where traditional gaskets would fail or leak.

Petrochemical Sector

High-pressure Gas/Liquid exchange and chemical heat recovery duties with high safety margins.

HVAC Ammonia Systems

Large-scale cooling for data centers and cold storage using efficient, sustainable refrigeration duties.
