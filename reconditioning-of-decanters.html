How We Recondition Decanters

A proven methodology for reliable and lasting results

01

Inspection & Dismantling

The decanter is fully dismantled; all components are inspected for wear, cracks, corrosion, and dimensional accuracy.

02

Cleaning & Surface Preparation

All parts are cleaned, grit-blasted, and prepared for repair to ensure strong adhesion and surface integrity.

03

Repair of Bowl, Conveyor & Gearbox

Worn areas are rebuilt, hardfaced, coated, and gearbox overhauled. Damaged components are repaired or replaced.

04

Balancing & Reassembly

Bowl and screw conveyor are dynamically balanced, then reassembled with new bearings, seals, and proper alignment.

05

Final Testing & Quality Check

Machine is test-run to verify vibration, performance, temperature, and differential speed before delivery.
