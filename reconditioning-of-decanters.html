Reconditioning of Decanters

// Service Overview

Restoring Decanter Performance to As-New Standards

Decanters subjected to years of heavy-duty operation accumulate wear across bowls, scroll conveyors, bearings, and mechanical seals. Rather than replacing capital-intensive equipment, Brigantine Engineering's reconditioning programme restores your decanter to OEM performance specifications at a fraction of replacement cost.

Our workshops are equipped with precision machining, dynamic balancing, and NDT inspection capability. Technicians apply OEM-approved procedures, genuine parts, and rigorous quality control at every stage.

Workshop

Fully equipped reconditioning workshop with precision machining capability

OEM Parts

Genuine Alfa Laval spare parts and approved alternative components

NDT Testing

Non-destructive weld and material integrity testing as standard

Certification

Full service documentation and performance certification on completion

// Reconditioning Scope

Technical Service Scope

Every component that affects decanter performance and reliability is addressed within our reconditioning programme. Nothing is left to chance.

01
Bowl & Scroll Reconditioning

Full disassembly, bore measurement, NDT inspection, refurbishment of discharge ports, and precision re-machining of seatings.

02
Bearing & Seal Overhaul

Replacement of main bearings and mechanical seals with genuine parts. Surface inspection and re-machining where required.

03
Gearbox Reconditioning

Full backdrive gearbox disassembly, gear inspection, and clearance verification before final reassembly and sign-off.

04
Dynamic Balancing

Precision two-plane dynamic balancing of bowl and scroll assemblies to OEM vibration limits for smooth high-speed operation.

05
Frame Refurbishment

Inspection and repair of the main frame, casing, and hood. Surface preparation and protective coating application.

06
Operational Validation

Full load testing and sequence verification to confirm equipment is returned to peak operational performance.

Local Australian Capability

We perform reconditioning within Australia, eliminating lengthy transit times and complexities of overseas overhaul.

  • Single-point accountability locally
